Everything you need to know before Prio launches.

Yes, free. Every part of Prio (natural language input, AI scheduling, timer, calendar, and projects) is free to download and use. No paywall and nothing locked behind an upgrade.

Prio is available on iPhone (iOS 16 and above) and Android (version 10 and above). There is no web version at this time.

When you add a task you can tell Prio the estimated time and difficulty. If you don't mention them, Prio uses your past patterns and the subject matter to make a smart estimate. You can adjust it at any time.

Your assignments and schedule are stored in the cloud with encryption at rest. We do not sell or share your personal data with anyone. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Prio works for both high school and college students. The more classes and commitments you're juggling, the more useful it becomes.
