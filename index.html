Procrastination

Without knowing what to work on and when, you fill your day with other things such as video games, scrolling, and wasting your time. By the time you realize you have an essay due tomorrow, you wished you had started working on it a week ago.

Our Solution

Prio solves all these problems. Once you add your task to the calendar, the app adds work blocks for the days leading up to your due date, which also show up on your calendar. This way, when you check your calendar when you sit down to work, you know exactly what you have to work on.

Prio is built by students who were tired of juggling six classes, practice, and a social life with tools that weren't built for them.

01

Just type it.

Compared to traditional calendars, you input your assignments to our smart AI in plain English, and the logic does the rest. This can save minutes trying to figure out buggy interfaces.

02

Your day, planned for you.

Prio arranges your home screen into an organized checklist of everything you need to do today, sorted by urgency and prioritizing your schedule first.

03

Stress-free calendar.

Normal calendars overwhelm you with your work right when you look at them. Ours has all the same functionality, but with a layout designed to tell you just what you need, and that's it.
